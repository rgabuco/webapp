Year Founded

Creative Studio Rentals was founded in 2024, with a mission to provide versatile and affordable creative spaces for artists, filmmakers, photographers, and other professionals.

Founders

The company was established by a group of passionate creatives, including Rudy Gabuco, Jensen Castro, and Dawn Bosing, who recognized the need for accessible studio spaces to support the growing creative community.

Location

Creative Studio Rentals is headquartered in Calgary, Alberta, with additional studio spaces available in Vancouver and Toronto, catering to the diverse creative scenes in these major cities.

Services Offered

The company offers a wide range of services, including studio rentals for photography, video production, and audio recording. They also provide equipment rentals, event spaces, and customized studio setups to meet the unique needs of their clients.
