Our Journey

Established in 2024, Creative Studio Rentals was born out of a passion for providing flexible and affordable spaces that cater to the unique needs of artists, filmmakers, photographers, and other creative professionals.

Meet the Founders

Our company was founded by a talented group of creatives—Rudy Gabuco, Jensen Castro, and Dawn Bosing—who shared a vision of making high-quality studio spaces accessible to everyone in the creative community.

Where We Are

Based in the vibrant city of Calgary, Alberta, Creative Studio Rentals has expanded to include additional locations in Vancouver and Toronto, each designed to serve the dynamic and diverse creative scenes across these major hubs.

What We Offer

We provide a comprehensive range of services, including studio rentals for photography, video production, and audio recording. Additionally, we offer equipment rentals, event spaces, and fully customizable studio setups tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.
